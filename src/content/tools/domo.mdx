Domo is a cloud-based business intelligence platform that helps organizations connect data from multiple sources, create interactive visualizations and dashboards, and generate AI-powered insights. The platform enables users to build data products, automate workflows, and share analytics across teams and customers. It's designed for business users, executives, and data analysts to make data-driven decisions through real-time dashboards accessible across devices.
