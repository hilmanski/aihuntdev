MiniMax is a multimodal AI platform that offers a comprehensive suite of foundation models for generating text, video, audio, images, and music. Developers can access their models through APIs, including the MiniMax M2 text model for coding and agentic tasks, Hailuo video generation for text-to-video and image-to-video conversion, Speech models for text-to-speech and voice cloning, and Music generation capabilities. The platform provides developer documentation, API access, and tools for building AI-native applications with over 200 million users worldwide.
