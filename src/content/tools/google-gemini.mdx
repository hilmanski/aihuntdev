Google Gemini is an AI assistant that provides help with writing, planning, brainstorming, and creative tasks. It can generate and edit images, create custom quizzes and flashcards, turn files into podcasts, and integrate with Google apps like Gmail, Docs, Sheets, YouTube, and Maps. The tool offers features like Gemini Live for real-time conversations, Canvas for creating apps and prototypes, Deep Research capabilities, and video generation. Users can access different tiers including free, Pro, and Ultra plans with varying levels of model access and features.
